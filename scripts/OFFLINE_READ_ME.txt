STUDYBUDDY COMPLETE OFFLINE — QUICK INSTALL

1. Double-click “Install StudyBuddy Offline.command”.
2. Confirm the installation in Terminal.
3. StudyBuddy and its private local AI are copied to ~/Applications.
4. The app opens automatically when installation finishes.

The first installation can take several minutes because the included model is
large. Keep at least 6 GB of free disk space. No internet connection is needed.

Your documents, notes, highlights, chats, RAG index, and reading progress stay
on this Mac. Apple or Google sign-in is optional and only personalizes the local
profile; cloud synchronization is disabled in this release.

If macOS blocks this development build, Control-click StudyBuddy, choose Open,
then choose Open once more. A signed and notarized public build will remove this
extra step.
